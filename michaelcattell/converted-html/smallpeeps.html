About the project

www.smallpeeps.com

“A group of passionate moms who want to find quality products.”

A great new start up of a friend of mine, selling all things for kids: from party favours to one off gifts.

One word – fun!

My Role

Design logo and personalize ‘off-the-shelf’ bought shop (for e-commerce). Also assist in teaching how to use backend of shop and advise on SEO techniques.

The Layout

From the previous layout, I decided it would be easier to do a more clean and simple approach to help the user buy more than one product, thus implementing featured products section and a carousel.

Is it a bird..well no, it’s a logo

The request was to do a logo which was ‘youthful but smart enough to go inside a boutique..oh and also a bird within the logo due to the naming of ‘Peeps’, of course I happily obliged – I haven’t drawn a bird in years!

Putting it all together

All that had to be done regarding the print side was update my previously designed business cards, product tags and complement slips (when ordered online) with the new logo. The website was more tricky, personalizing quite a rigid template with tiny improvements (carousel, free postage sticker, featured products section and making it look cuter) alongside a quite tutorial on best practices and use of the admin section.
