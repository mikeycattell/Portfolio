About the project

www.argosonline.es

Argos is the UK's leading digital and in store retailer, I am sure sometime in your childhood you have been in there – trying to work out how many pens you can cram into your pocket I got 5 before my mum noticed!

On average the stores (740 of them) and online services, serves around 130 million customers a year.

The need was to design and build a website, answering to the Spain users of Argos (Ex Pats to be exact).

My Role

Be the Front-end Developer, working alongside their marketing team to ensure consistent branding throughout the home and sub pages.

The Layout

Before setting out to build the site, we designed a plan of action, so to fall under the branding rules – to ensure consistent branding was kept. The famous red of Argos’ logo was to be used often to offset the blue that is linked with the company since INSERT YR.

Location Location Location

As the site was aiming to Ex-pat audience, we kept the feeling of Argos intact while promoting more diverse products – who doesn’t want a BBQ or sun beds in Spain?

Putting it all together

Build (I didn’t design the complete site, but had to do sub-pages taken from rough storyboarding) took 5 days, from start to finish, built within a pre-existing PHP environment.
