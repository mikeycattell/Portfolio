Meet me

Well hello there, I’m Michael (often known as Mikey), for those of you that I haven’t met yet, I’m a friendly and often funny User Interface Designer and Front-end Developer from England, currently living in California, America.

I am an experienced designer, with a love to travel and go where I'm needed to help people build product, this adaptability allows me to work with small to big agencies and start ups on a freelance basis based in UK and America, to assist in making the most of their web presence.

As of November 2013, I will be joining the world of freelance design and build, focusing on designing and developing experiences, so feel free to get in touch to discuss your plans and excited!
