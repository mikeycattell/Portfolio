Me in a brief sentence: Freelance Designer & Front End Developer. Husband to Emma, father to a rather fluffy Pomeranian called Ralph.

My experience includes being in this industry for 9 years, working for agencies, startups, huge corporations and now, as a freelancer - based in Yorkshire, Birmingham and California, America (sometimes remotely, sometimes on location).

Specialising in Front end web design, ecommerce & interface design plus the occasional app design. Also have a keen practiced interest in branding and logo design.

I mainly work from my well equipped offices based in Doncaster and Birmingham. When I’m not my office I’m usually on-site with clients or agencies around Yorkshire, Midlands or even America!

You’ll usually find me with Photoshop or (the mighty) Fireworks open, hard-coding HTML5 responsive websites like I’m a man obsessed, making magical things happen with CSS3 or chasing my little Ralph around the garden with my camera!
